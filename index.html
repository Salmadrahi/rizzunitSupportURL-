Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I manage or cancel my Pro subscription?

All subscriptions are managed directly by Apple. To cancel or change your Rizz Unit Pro subscription (Weekly or Monthly), open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, tap Subscriptions, and select Rizz Unit to make changes.

I upgraded to Pro but my app is still locked. What do I do?

Sometimes your device needs to resync with the App Store. Open Rizz Unit, go to the Pro upgrade screen, and tap the Restore Purchases button. This will verify your active subscription without charging you again.

How do I change the language of the AI replies?

Rizz Unit supports English, Arabic, French, Spanish, Turkish, Urdu, and Portuguese. You can change your preferred language directly within the app settings before generating a new reply.

Is my data safe?

Yes. The screenshots and text you upload are securely transmitted strictly to generate the AI response and are not permanently stored. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
